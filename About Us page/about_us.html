Women are constantly plagued with uncertainties and worries about their safety when riding alone in a male-driven cab, and women drivers are always on the edge when driving around male passengers. The involvement of women in the transport sector is meagre when compared to men.
BackFeaturesTo eliminate this disparity, we have come up with an idea that allows women to travel safely, in cabs and taxis driven by other women. In order to encourage women in this sector, we provide driver jobs only for women. This can also be used for the safe travel of children below the age of 18 years.
